# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

> A comprehensive Power BI dashboard to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution — enabling data-driven decisions for operational efficiency and business growth.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

To conduct a thorough analysis of Blinkit's key business metrics using Power BI, covering:

- **Sales Performance** — tracking revenue trends and growth patterns
- **Customer Satisfaction** — evaluating ratings and feedback data
- **Inventory Distribution** — monitoring stock allocation across categories

The goal is to surface actionable insights and optimization opportunities that drive business growth.

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1. Requirement Gathering / Business Understanding
Engaged with stakeholders to define business objectives, success metrics, and the overall scope of the analysis.

### 2. Data Collection & Walkthrough
Collected and reviewed datasets covering sales transactions, customer satisfaction scores, and inventory records.

### 3. Data Connection
Established live connections between Power BI Desktop and the relevant data sources for seamless data ingestion.

### 4. Data Cleaning & Quality Check
- Removed duplicates and null values
- Standardized formats across date, category, and numeric fields
- Validated data integrity before modeling

### 5. Data Modeling
Designed a structured data model (star/snowflake schema) to enable efficient relationships between tables for analysis.

### 6. Data Processing
Transformed raw data into derived metrics and trend indicators to support visualization needs.

### 7. DAX Calculations
Applied DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) to compute KPIs including:
- Total Sales & Sales Growth (%)
- Average Customer Satisfaction Score
- Inventory Turnover Rate
- Category-wise Revenue Contribution

### 8. Dashboard Layout Design
Planned a user-friendly dashboard layout with logical information flow, ensuring clarity and ease of navigation for stakeholders.

### 9. Charts Development & Formatting
Built dynamic and interactive visualizations including:
- Sales trend line charts
- Category-wise bar/column charts
- Customer satisfaction gauges
- Inventory heatmaps and distribution charts

### 10. Dashboard & Report Development
Developed the final Power BI report featuring:
- Real-time data refresh capability
- Slicers and filters for interactive exploration
- Drill-through pages for granular analysis
- Actionable insight callouts

### 11. Insights Generation
Extracted key business insights from the dashboard to guide strategic decision-making around sales improvement, customer experience, and inventory management.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Tracked

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Total Sales | Overall revenue generated across all categories |
| Sales Growth (%) | Period-over-period growth rate |
| Customer Satisfaction Score | Average rating from customer feedback |
| Inventory Turnover | Rate at which inventory is sold and replenished |
| Category Performance | Sales contribution by product category |
